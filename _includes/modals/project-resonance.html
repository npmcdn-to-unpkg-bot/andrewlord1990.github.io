Resonance

A co-operative multi-player game developed for the Xbox 360

It is a co-operative game with one player controlling the movement of the on-screen character using a standard Xbox 360 controller, while the other uses the drum kit controller to attack the enemies. It combines action with music-based rhythmic game-play. The game was developed as part of a team of six at university.
