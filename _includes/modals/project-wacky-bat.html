Wacky Bat: Swoop Outta Dodge

An endless 2D mobile arcade game, with increasing difficulty and simple controls

An addictive free-to-play mobile game, that uses simple one-touch controls. The aim is to fly as far as you can, collecting fruit along the way whilst avoiding a variety of obstacles and trying to beat your previous score.

Features include: obstacle spawning using changing probabilities; Google Play Games/Game Center integration; sound and particle effects; increasing difficulty and a custom developed menu system.

Please see the Blast Cap Games website for more information.

  • Language: C#, Unity3D
  • Platform: Android, iOS
